Welcome to Muay Thai of Denver!

Our gym is more than just a place to train; it's a community where individuals come together to learn, grow, and support one another on their martial arts journey.

Founded by passionate martial artists, Muay Thai of Denver is dedicated to providing high-quality training in the art of Muay Thai. Our experienced instructors bring years of experience and expertise to every class, ensuring that each student receives personalized attention and guidance.

At Muay Thai of Denver, we believe in the power of martial arts to transform lives. We welcome students of all ages and skill levels, from beginners to seasoned fighters. But Muay Thai of Denver is more than just a gym; it's a community. Our students come from all walks of life, united by their passion for martial arts. Together, we train hard, support each other, and celebrate our achievements both inside and outside the gym.

Join us at Muay Thai of Denver and discover the joy of training in a supportive and inclusive environment. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fighter, you'll find a home here. Come train with us and be part of something special!

Meet Our Head Coach

Join us in welcoming our team of skilled coaches who bring years of experience and expertise to Muay Thai of Denver. Our head coach, Mark Anderson, is a certified Muay Thai instructor with over a decade of training and teaching experience. He holds multiple championship titles and has trained under some of the most renowned Muay Thai masters in Thailand.
